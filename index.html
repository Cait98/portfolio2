About Me

I've always had a passion for something in the creative field and was unsure of what path to take until I discovered level 2 design and essentially loved the work process and the creative nature of the work that I decided to further myself and study it at university. My goal in studying visual communication at Massey is to gain insight into a field that excites me and a field in which many artforms can be explored and implemented. Another main goal of mine is to have a philanthropic approach to design in which I wish to design for charities and various organizations working on actively making a difference in the world.

I am constantly influenced by my surroundings and numerous visual mediums such as Pinterest, Facebook, Instagram, magazines, T.V, books etc; These all act as a means of inspiration and help me render ideas and different approaches to my designs.

Apart from design I also like exploring new surroundings, going for walks, taking photographs, watching countless movies, listening to numerous different genres of music and spending time with family and friends.
